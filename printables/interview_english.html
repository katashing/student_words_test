英文面试问题

根据 2026-05-11 老师面试题和孩子作答整理。绿色为建议表达，适合孩子复习和老师/家长批改参考。

一、自我介绍与个人信息

#老师问题孩子原答建议表达中文提示
1What is your name?My name is 若飏. My English name is Ryan. You can call me Ryan if you wish.My name is Wu Ruoyang. My English name is Ryan. You can call me Ryan.说明姓名和英文名，最后补一句可以怎么称呼。
2How old are you?I am 8 years old.I am eight years old.年龄可以用英文数字完整表达。
3Where do you study now? / Which school do you go to?I study at Hanlin Primary School. I am in grade 2.I study at Hanlin Primary School. I am in Grade Two.学校 + 年级，两句话清楚回答。
4Do you have a nickname? / What can I call you?My nickname is Qiqi.My nickname is Qiqi, so you can call me Qiqi.可补充一句对方可以怎么称呼你。

二、家庭与生活

#老师问题孩子原答建议表达中文提示
1How many people are there in your family?There are 4 people in my family. They are my dad, mum, brother and me.There are four people in my family: my dad, my mum, my brother, and me.人数 + 家庭成员，注意列举顺序和 and。
2Who takes care of your studies at home? / Who helps you with your homework?My parents help with my homework.My parents help me with my homework at home.help me with my homework 是更自然的表达。
3What do you like to do with your family on weekends?I like to fly a kite in the park with my family on weekends.On weekends, I like to fly a kite in the park with my family.时间状语放前面，句子更顺。

三、兴趣与爱好

#老师问题孩子原答建议表达中文提示
1What is your hobby? / What do you like to do in your free time?My hobbies are reading books, such as Journey to the West, and riding a bike.My hobbies are reading books and riding a bike. I like reading Journey to the West.爱好多时用 hobbies；书名单独补一句更清楚。
2Do you like sports? / What sport do you like?I like swimming, because swimming is good for my health.Yes, I like swimming because it is good for my health.先回答 Yes，再说明运动和原因。
3Do you like drawing / singing / dancing?No, I don't like dancing, but I like drawing.I don't like dancing, but I like drawing.but 连接不喜欢和喜欢的内容。
4What is your favorite toy? Why?My favorite toy are my toy cars. They are my birthday gifts.My favorite toys are my toy cars because they were birthday gifts.toy cars 是复数，前面用 toys are。

四、学习与阅读

#老师问题孩子原答建议表达中文提示
1What is your favorite subject? Why?My favorite subject is English, because English is useful. We can talk to people around in English.My favorite subject is English because it is useful. We can use English to talk to people around the world.原因后面补充 English 的用途。
2Do you like reading?Yes, I like Journey to the West. It is my favorite book.Yes, I like reading. My favorite book is Journey to the West.先回答是否喜欢阅读，再说最喜欢的书。

练习重点

  • 回答尽量用完整句，不只说一个词。
  • 注意单复数：toy cars / toys are。
  • 先直接回答，再用 because 补充原因。
  • 阅读题先说 Yes, I like reading，再介绍 favorite book。
